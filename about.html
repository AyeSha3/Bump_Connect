  • About Us

Bump Connect is a nonprofit organization that promotes a natural, healthy and safe approach to pregnancy, childbirth and early parenting. Knowing that pregnancy and childbirth can be demanding on a woman’s body and mind, Bump Connect serves as a resource for information about what to expect and what choices are available during the childbearing years.

At Bump Connect, we aim to empower new parents and parents-to-be with expert content and advice.
