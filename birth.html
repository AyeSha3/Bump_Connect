Life in the womb

Boy or Girl?Learn more

If your hair growth is higher than the normal growth then you are more likely to have a baby boy.

If your urine is a dull yellow, plan on a girl or if it is bright yellow it is a boy.

Sugar Cravings = Girl

During pregnancy if your foot is always colder then it is more likely a baby boy.

Clumsier than usual = Boy

If you have morning sickness that seems to last all day long and last for longer than your first trimester, then you are having a girl.

Low Baby Bump = Boy

If your hand is very dry and cracked then it may be a boy.

Always on the toilet = Girl

If your baby's heart rate is above 140 then it's a girl and if below then it's a boy.

Birth Pratices

Regardless of your baby’s size, your labor’s length and complexity, or your confidence level, these care practices will help keep labor and your baby's birth as safe and healthy as possible.
